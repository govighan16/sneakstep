About Us

SneakStep is a leading online destination for shoe enthusiasts, offering a wide range of footwear from renowned brands, including Nike, Adidas, and many more. As a one-stop shop for sneaker aficionados, SneakStep provides a diverse collection of shoes that cater to various tastes and preferences, ensuring that customers can find the perfect pair for any occasion or style.

What sets SneakStep apart is our commitment to offering not just shoes but also unforgettable experiences. In an exciting development, we are proud to announce an exclusive partnership with Neymar Jr, the iconic Brazilian football superstar. This groundbreaking collaboration has given rise to a unique line of football shoes that reflect Neymar Jr's distinctive playing style and personality.

These exclusive football shoes, born from our partnership with Neymar Jr, are a testament to our dedication to merging style and functionality. They offer both fans and athletes the opportunity to experience a piece of his world-class performance on the field. At SneakStep, we believe in providing footwear that goes beyond the ordinary, and our partnership with Neymar Jr is a testament to this commitment.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Sneak Step?

Sneak Step is a leading footwear company specializing in stylish and comfortable sneakers for both men and women.

How can I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our website, select the sneakers you like, choose your size, and add them to your cart. Then, proceed to checkout and follow the steps to complete your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

Sneak Step accepts a variety of payment methods, including credit and debit cards, PayPal and also crypto currency exchange.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, Sneak Step offers international shipping. We ship to various countries around the world. Shipping fees and delivery times may vary depending on your location.

What is your return policy?

Our return policy allows you to return sneakers within 30 days of purchase. They must be in unworn and original condition. Please visit our Returns page for more details.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your Sneak Step account and visiting the Order Tracking page. You'll find real-time updates on your order's status.

Are your sneakers available in physical stores?

No, unfortunately as of this moment, SneakStep is an online only store. However, look out in the future if there are any near you.

How long does delivery take?

We have the option of premium express delivery which aims to deliver purchases within 1-3 days, based on location. There is also free delivery for orders above £40 which takes at most 7 working days to deliver.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team by visiting our Contact Us page. We offer various contact methods, including email and phone support.
